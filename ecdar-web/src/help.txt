Usage: ecdar-web [Options]

-r OR --root  <folder>  -- folder being the root folder of the build of Ecdar-GUI-Web.
                        |  Default = .

-s OR --serve <ip:port> -- where and what port to serve the server. 
                        |  Default = 0.0.0.0:3000  -- means localhost port 3000.

-h OR --help            -- Opens this message
